# 🌿 MindWell — Mental Health & Wellness Website

A beautiful, calming mental health and wellness website to help you take care of your mind every day.

## ✨ Features

- 🧘 Breathing Exercise — Guided box breathing animation
- 📓 Gratitude Journal — Write and save what you're grateful for
- 😊 Mood Tracker — Log your daily mood with emoji selection
- 💬 Positive Affirmations — Random uplifting affirmations
- 🎵 Calm Sounds — Ambient sounds for relaxation
- 📋 Daily Self-Care Checklist — Track your wellness habits
- 🆘 Mental Health Resources — Helplines and support links
- 🌙 Dark/Light Mode — Comfortable viewing anytime

## 🚀 How to Use

Simply open `index.html` in your browser. No build tools, frameworks, or servers needed!

## 💛 A Note on Mental Health

Your mental health matters. This website is a small tool to support your daily wellness journey. Remember — it's okay to ask for help. You are not alone. 💛
